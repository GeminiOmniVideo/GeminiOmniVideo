## 🎬 What is Gemini Omni Video?

**[Gemini Omni Video AI](https://geminiomnivideo.ai)** is a conversational AI video generator built on Google's Gemini Omni model. Instead of dragging clips on a timeline, you direct your video through dialogue. Describe a scene, ask for changes, and watch the AI re-render in real time. It's video production reimagined as a creative conversation.

*   **Direct with Words:** Give instructions the way you'd brief a human editor — 'make the lighting warmer,' 'slow the camera pan,' 'add rain to the background.' The AI understands intent, not just keywords.
*   **Any Input, One Workflow:** Feed it a script, a product photo, a voice memo, or all three at once. Everything merges into a single coherent video without manual compositing.
*   **From Draft to Final Cut:** Every generation is a starting point, not a dead end. Iterate shot by shot until the output matches your vision.

## 🚀 How It Works

Three moves from blank canvas to finished video:

1.  **Set the Stage**
    Drop in your raw materials — a text script, reference photos, audio clips, or any mix. [Gemini Omni Video](https://geminiomnivideo.ai) reads everything together and builds a unified creative brief automatically.
2.  **Direct the Scene**
    Tell the AI what you want: camera angle, pacing, color grade, mood. Be vague or be precise — the AI adapts to your level of detail and fills in the rest intelligently.
3.  **Refine Until Perfect**
    Preview your video and give feedback in plain language — 'make it moodier,' 'cut the intro shorter,' 'zoom in on the product.' Export in 4K when you're happy.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### How is it different from other AI video tools?
Most AI video generators are one-shot: you prompt, you get a clip, and if it's wrong you start over. **[Gemini Omni AI Video](https://geminiomnivideo.ai)** keeps session context so you can refine any detail — lighting, framing, pacing, dialogue — through follow-up messages without losing prior work.

### What inputs are accepted?
Text prompts, images (PNG, JPG, WebP), audio files, and video clips — in any combination. You can feed it a product photo with a voiceover script, or just type a one-line idea.

### What formats and resolutions are supported?
Exports up to 4K resolution in 16:9 (landscape), 9:16 (portrait), and 1:1 (square). Duration ranges from 4 to 15 seconds per clip, with multi-clip projects supported through conversation.

### Can I use the output commercially?
Yes. All videos generated come with full commercial usage rights for ads, social media, client work, and product pages — no additional licensing required.

### Is this affiliated with Google?
**[Gemini Omni Video AI](https://geminiomnivideo.ai)** is built on Google's Gemini Omni model and provides an optimized creative workspace for video generation. It is an independent product focused on making AI video creation accessible to everyone.
